chef. Kevin Zapata

Chef Kevin Zapata is a world-renowned patissier whose passion for pastry has defined his career.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep love for the art of baking, he has mastered the delicate balance of flavor, texture, and presentation. Driven by his desire to share his creations with the world, Chef Kevin founded "Whisk," a local bakery that quickly gained a reputation for excellence.

Whisk offers a variety of world-class baked goods, from delicate pastries to artisan breads, each crafted with precision and care. Whether it's a special occasion or a daily indulgence, Whisk has something for everyone, turning the ordinary into the extraordinary with every bite.

Chef Kevin's commitment to quality and innovation ensures that every creation is a celebration of flavor, making Whisk a beloved destination for pastry lovers everywhere.
